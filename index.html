008 / Key Takeaways

YOUR ACTION
PLAN

Prioritize sleep above everything. Quality 8-hour sleep is the foundation. You can't focus without it. You can't perform without it. Start here.

Block 2-3 deep work zones daily. No meetings, no interruptions, no phone. 9:30 AM to 1 PM is sacred. Protect it like your life depends on it.

Focus on RPA, not random tasks. 10 result-providing activities beat 100 busy-work tasks. Ask: "Does this move the needle?" If not, drop it.

Measure everything. Sleep, focus blocks, exercise, business metrics. Convert life into numbers. What gets measured gets improved.

Use the 168-hour weekly planner. Map your constraints first. Then fill in deep focus zones. Plan the week scientifically, not reactively.

Let AI challenge you, not validate you. Set up your AI as a brutal, honest advisor. Give it your constraints. Let it question your decisions.

Create your daily scorecard. Track completion. Score yourself. When you see the game, it becomes engaging. Like an IPL match with a live scoreboard.

Focus creates time. Measurement creates growth.
